About me

I worked in conservation and environment programs as a monitoring and evaluation officer. I conducted research-related works, including data collection, method design, analytics, and visualization.

In 2018, I pursued the executive Master of Development Policies and Practices (DPP) at the Graduate institute, Geneva.

After that, I joined several studies with my former supervisor and colleagues

I focus on environmental policies’ causalities, which could determine a long-term impact. I strive to study some questions like ‘whether the carbon payment can treat beneficiaries neutral?’.
