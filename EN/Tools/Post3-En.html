Applied Awesome Table and Google Suite for Creating Basic Reporting Tool

Posted by Nhung Le on Oct 30, 2018

When I started as a junior Monitoring and Evaluation assistant, most of the time I work  is reviewing number of solutions for designing monitoring information system to collect data at different levels. Ideal solution needs to adapt with different requirements: flexibility, easy to design fields of information, time and cost saving for set-up. Especially, the technology for setting up the system is one key concern to design a monitoring information system.

 

I have studied and tried different solutions to find the solutions whether fit in with our requirements. Each of them has different pros and cons to use. One of those is Awesome Table, a cross cutting solution to match with what we looking for (Flexible, Easy to use, Free cost). Moreover, It is based on G-Suite for displaying data from Google sheets. In my organization, we often work with Google sheets, forms to collect data in case of timely reporting from number of field offices.  However, google sheets has number of limitations including the display of information which Awesome table helps to solve the issue.

 

The numbers of examples for applying Awesome Table to display information in different uses (not only Monitoring Information System). What you need is a datasheet (Google sheet, Excel sheet) you can show the information in the interactive and dynamic way
